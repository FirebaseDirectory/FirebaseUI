Apps in minutes

FirebaseUI takes all the power and speed of Firebase and binds it to common native UI controls on Android and iOS, so you can focus on building content rather than writing boilerplate code.

FirebaseUI replaces redundent logic, simplifies view controller code, and accelerates development, meaning that you have more time to focus on how the app looks and feels.

FirebaseUI features

Common controls

Bind ListViews, GridViews, and RecyclerViews or UITableViews and UICollectionViews to Firebase queries.

Design independent

FirebaseUI isn't oppinionated about your chosen design style; we do the heavy lifting, you focus on making your app look great.

Fully open source

All code and examples are open for you to learn from, build on, and contribute back to. Issues and pull requests welcome!
